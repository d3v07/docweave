ACME CORPORATION ANNOUNCES RECORD Q4 RESULTS AND NEW PRODUCT LAUNCH

San Francisco, CA - Acme Corporation (NASDAQ: ACME) today announced record fourth quarter results and the upcoming launch of its highly anticipated Widget Pro product line.

FINANCIAL HIGHLIGHTS
- Q4 Revenue: $1.4 billion, up 23% year-over-year
- Full Year Revenue: $5.2 billion, up 18% year-over-year
- Operating Margin: 24.5%
- Employee Count: 12,500 worldwide

"We are thrilled to close out the year with such strong results," said John Smith, Chief Executive Officer of Acme Corporation. "Our team's dedication to innovation and customer success continues to drive our growth."

WIDGET PRO LAUNCH
Acme also announced that Widget Pro, its next-generation productivity solution, will launch on February 15, 2024. Widget Pro features advanced AI capabilities and seamless cloud integration.

Key Features:
- AI-powered automation
- Real-time collaboration
- Enterprise-grade security
- Cross-platform compatibility

Widget Pro will be available for $299 for individual users and custom enterprise pricing.

ABOUT ACME CORPORATION
Acme Corporation is a leading provider of enterprise software solutions, serving over 10,000 customers globally. Founded in 2005, Acme is headquartered in San Francisco with offices in New York, London, and Tokyo.
